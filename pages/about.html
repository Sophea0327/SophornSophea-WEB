About Book Heaven

Book Heaven is a local online bookshop dedicated to bringing Khmer and international books to readers everywhere — from timeless classics to modern bestsellers, curated collections, and author features.

Founded with the belief that books change lives, we combine a carefully curated catalog, trusted delivery, and community events to make reading enjoyable and accessible.

Contact Us

Established: 2022

Located in Phnom Penh — shipping nationwide

Our Mission

To cultivate a joyful reading culture in Cambodia by connecting readers with diverse stories, local authors, and affordable books — both physical and digital.

Our Vision

To be the most-loved book destination for Khmer readers and learners — recognized for curation, fair pricing, and community impact.

Core Values

Community • Quality • Accessibility • Integrity

Our Story

From a small stall to a digital store, a quick timeline of how Book Heaven grew:

2020

Humble Beginnings

Started as a weekend book stall in a local market with a handpicked selection of Khmer titles.

2021

Online Launch

Launched Book Heaven website and began shipping across the country.

2022

Growth & Community

Started author events, reading clubs, and partnerships with schools.

2024

Today

Serving thousands of readers with curated collections and a growing Khmer catalog.
