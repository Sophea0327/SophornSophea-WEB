About Book Heaven

Book Heaven is a local online bookshop dedicated to bringing Khmer and international books to readers everywhere from timeless classics to modern bestsellers, curated collections, and author features.

Founded with the belief that books change lives, we combine a carefully curated catalog, trusted delivery, and community events to make reading enjoyable and accessible.
